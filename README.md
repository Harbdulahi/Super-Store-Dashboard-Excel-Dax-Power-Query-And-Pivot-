# Superstore Sales Performance Dashboard (Excel)

## Overview
This project analyzes Superstore sales data using Microsoft Excel to track revenue, profit, quantity sold, customer distribution, and geographical performance. The dashboard is designed to give a fast, executive-level view of business performance across time, product categories, and U.S. states.

---

## Objective
Answer core business questions:
- How much revenue and profit is the store generating?
- How does performance compare year-over-year?
- How do sales trend across months?
- Which product categories generate the most revenue and profit?
- Which states drive the highest sales?
- How many active customers contribute to revenue?

---

## Dataset
**Superstore Sales Dataset**

Common fields:
- Order Date
- Customer Name
- Segment (Consumer, Corporate, Home Office)
- Category
- Sub-Category
- Sales
- Profit
- Quantity
- State

Data is cleaned and modeled directly in Excel.

---

## Key Metrics
- Total Revenue
- Total Profit
- Total Quantity Sold
- Profit Margin
- Customer Count
- Year-over-Year Growth (%)

---

## Insights (Examples)
- Technology generates the highest revenue and profit.
- California and New York dominate total sales.
- Sales peak toward the end of the year.
- Profit growth outpaces revenue growth year-over-year.
- Some categories show high revenue but lower margins.

---

## Limitations
- Analysis limited to historical data.
- Excel-based model is not optimized for very large datasets.
- No automated refresh or forecasting.
